Why do I have to pre-pay?

We sell loaves by walk-up and pre-order. Since we typically sell out, we often have to turn away walk-up customers. If people don't pre-pay, then we lose money every time someone forgets to pick up their bread, because we turned away a walk-up customer who would have paid for it.

What if I forgot to pick up my bread?

This actually happens a lot. We're sorry, but if you haven't picked up your bread by 7 pm (or made other arrangements), then your bread will be given away. We can't offer a refund or future credit, because we would lose too much money.

Why is your bread so expensive!?!?

I'm glad you asked! There are a few reasons:

1) We only use organic, artisinal flour, which is not cheap!
2) Making bread takes time! We hand mix, scale, shape, and bake all of our loaves in a wood fired oven. The whole process takes around 6-10 person-hours, and spans two days!

It really boils down to this: We love baking, but we need to be able to make at least minimum wage to justify selling bread.
